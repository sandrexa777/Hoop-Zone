Coaching Corner

Fundamentals & Drills

Every great player is built on the basics. Master these pillars before you chase the highlights.

The Four Pillars

Build Your Game on These

01

Dribbling

Keep your eyes up, use your fingertips, and protect the ball with your off-hand. Control comes before speed.

02

Shooting

Balance, elbow under the ball, smooth follow-through. Repeat the same form until it's automatic — B.E.E.F.

03

Passing

The fastest way to move the ball is a crisp pass. Chest, bounce, and overhead — know when to use each.

04

Defense

Stay low, move your feet, and keep your hands active. Great defense travels with you every single night.

Practice Plan

Three Drills to Start Today

No gym? No problem. These build real skill with just a ball and a hoop.

Ball Handling

Figure-8 Dribble

Weave the ball between your legs in a figure-8 pattern. 60 seconds, eyes up, then switch directions.

⏱ 5 min · Beginner
Shooting

Form Shooting

Start one foot from the rim. Make 10 perfect shots, then take a step back. Quality over quantity.

⏱ 10 min · All levels
Conditioning

Suicide Sprints

Sprint to each line and back, increasing distance each time. Builds the engine for the fourth quarter.

⏱ 8 min · Intermediate
