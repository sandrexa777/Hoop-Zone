The Game

What Basketball Is All About

Basketball is a fast, fluid team sport where two teams of five compete to score by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop. It rewards skill, conditioning, smarts, and chemistry in equal measure.

⏱️

The Format

Games run over four quarters. The team with the most points when the clock hits zero wins. Tied? You go to overtime.

🎯

Scoring

Two points inside the arc, three from beyond it, and one for each free throw earned after a foul.

🤝

The Spirit

Above all, basketball is a team game. Spacing, passing, and trust beat raw talent almost every time.
