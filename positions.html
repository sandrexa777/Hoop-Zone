Know Your Role

The Five Positions

Each spot on the floor carries a job. Find where your game fits best.

PG

Point Guard

The floor general. Runs the offense, sets the tempo, and finds open teammates with sharp passing and vision.

SG

Shooting Guard

The scorer. Lives on the perimeter and knocks down jumpers off the catch and off the dribble.

SF

Small Forward

The all-rounder. Scores, defends, and rebounds — a versatile wing who can do a bit of everything.

PF

Power Forward

The workhorse. Battles inside, sets screens, and cleans the glass on both ends of the floor.

C

Center

The anchor. Protects the rim, finishes inside, and controls the paint with size and timing.
